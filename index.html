SPACE INVADERS!

Lives: 3

Score: 0

Rules of Engagement

Use the left and right arrows to move your gun and the up arrow to shoot. The aim is get rid of all aliens on the grid.

If you are hit by a bomb, you will lose a life.

If you hit an alien with a laser, you will score 150 points.

You can only have one laser in play at a time, so use it wisely!

Good luck!
